FAQ

What happens if Wisdom doesn't have the experts I'm looking for?

We'll find and onboard the right experts onto our platform if we don't already have them. Just email us at business@getwisdomapp.com to let us know what you're looking for

What does Wisdom do for compliance?

Every expert signs our terms and conditions. We ensure experts comply with any agreements or policies to which they are subject to; won't participate in surveys that would be in violation of such agreements or any obligations to third parties; will not share confidential information.

What happens if I'm not happy with answers I receive?

If you're not happy with an answer you've received, you don't have to pay for it. Only pay for information that you find useful
